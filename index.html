Our Mission

Las Vegas Robotics is dedicated to creating a thriving ecosystem where robotics enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators can connect, collaborate, and build the future of robotics.

Whether you're a founder looking to start a robotics company, an engineer seeking collaboration, an academic pursuing research, or an industry professional wanting to contribute, our community provides the platform and resources to make it happen.
