Our Programs

Empowering the next generation of robotics innovators

🎓

Fellowship Program

Connecting talented high school FIRST Robotics students with paid internships at leading tech companies. Our fellows gain real-world experience building robotics systems, developing software, and working alongside industry professionals.

Through mentorship and hands-on projects, students develop critical skills in engineering, programming, and professional development while earning competitive compensation.

Paid Internships Mentorship FIRST Robotics
🔧

Community Workshops

Regular hands-on workshops covering robotics fundamentals, advanced programming, mechanical design, and emerging technologies. Open to all skill levels from beginners to experienced builders.

Learn from industry experts and collaborate with fellow robotics enthusiasts in our state-of-the-art maker space.

All Levels Hands-On Free
🏆

Competition Support

Supporting local FIRST Robotics teams with resources, mentorship, and technical expertise. We help teams prepare for regional and national competitions through practice sessions and strategy development.

Our experienced mentors provide guidance on everything from mechanical design to autonomous programming.
